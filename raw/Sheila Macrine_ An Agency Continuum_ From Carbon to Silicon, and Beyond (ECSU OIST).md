ECogS Conference  
  
On 10th of December 2025, Sheila Macrine gave a talk as part of the ECogS Conference hosted by The Embodied Cognitive Science Unit at OIST.  
  
Title: An Agency Continuum: From Carbon to Silicon, and Beyond  
  
Abstract: This presentation, "An Agency Continuum," proposes a transdisciplinary framework for understanding the many profiles of agency. We move beyond the binary "Is it an agent?" question by introducing a multi-dimensional toolkit to deconstruct agency into nine core, measurable dimensions. We conclude by exploring the framework's broad implications, including its ability to provide a unified model of agential failure across scales (from cancer to psychosis), its clarification of embodiment as a necessary path for AGI , and its call for a substrate-agnostic, multi-scale science of agency.
