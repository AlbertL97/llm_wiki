# ManagingLife Unveils Solace, the First AI Companion Providing Evidence-Based Pain Psychology Support

**Source:** PR Newswire

**Date:** Not explicitly stated in the snippet, but implied to be recent.

## Summary

ManagingLife has introduced "Solace," an AI companion that offers evidence-based pain psychology support. This marks the first AI companion of its kind specifically developed to assist individuals in managing chronic pain. Solace integrates therapeutic principles into its design, aiming to provide accessible and effective support for users experiencing pain.

## Key Details:

*   **Product:** Solace
*   **Developer:** ManagingLife
*   **Key Feature:** First AI companion offering evidence-based pain psychology support.
*   **Purpose:** To help individuals manage chronic pain through AI-driven therapeutic guidance.
*   **Significance:** Represents a new frontier in AI applications for mental health and chronic condition management.
