### Title
Co-Creativity at the Table: A Qualitative Analysis of Creative Interactions in the Podcast "Adventure AI"

### URL
http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18010v1

### Summary
This paper details a qualitative analysis conducted on three seasons (2023-2025) of the podcast "Adventure AI," which features human-AI interactions in the context of Dungeons & Dragons tabletop role-playing games. The primary goal of the research was to investigate the practical applications and perceptions of AI in this complex and collaborative gaming environment. The analysis identified several overarching themes: the defined roles of AI within the game, the corresponding roles of human players, the evaluation of AI performance (including its successes and notable failures), and how AI is treated—specifically, its perception as a person or character by human participants at the table. The findings highlight specific areas where artificial intelligence demonstrates significant capability in gaming and other areas where its current application is less suitable. This research aims to provide a foundational basis for future work exploring optimal integration and usage of AI in collaborative and creative gaming spaces, with a direct focus on the psychological aspects of human-AI interaction and anthropomorphism.
