## Main content

### Overview
This Reuters report covers the launch of **Humanity's Last Exam (HLE)** by the Center for AI Safety (CAIS) and Scale AI. It outlines the rapid saturation of existing benchmarks by models like OpenAI's o1 (source: AI experts ready 'Humanity's Last Exam' to stump powerful tech.md).

### Key Themes
1. **Benchmark Saturation**: Dan Hendrycks (CAIS director) notes that benchmarks created in 2021 (testing undergraduate-level knowledge and math) have been 'crushed' by frontier models.
2. **Goal of HLE**: To crowdsource highly complex, expert-level questions from global specialists to test AGI capabilities, ensuring the test remains relevant as AI reasoning capabilities scale.
3. **Abstract Reasoning**: Unlike simple fact retrieval, HLE targets advanced abstract reasoning and cognitive synthesis, pushing the boundaries of what is considered expert-level human intelligence (source: AI experts ready 'Humanity's Last Exam' to stump powerful tech.md).
