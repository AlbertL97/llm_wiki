### 1. Target Sectors for Service Robotics
United Robotics Group deploys service robots in several professional areas (source: United Robotics Group _ Service robotics for your business.md):
- **Life Science**: Automates routine lab tasks to ensure precision, maintain safety, and allow researchers to focus on core tasks (source: United Robotics Group _ Service robotics for your business.md).
- **Healthcare**: Assists front-line and operational medical staff to streamline patient care and manage logistics (source: United Robotics Group _ Service robotics for your business.md).
- **Inspection & Maintenance**: Supports security and maintenance teams by executing inspections, patrols, and repairs in industrial environments (source: United Robotics Group _ Service robotics for your business.md).
- **Intralogistics**: Automates materials transportation and sorting on manufacturing and warehouse floors (source: United Robotics Group _ Service robotics for your business.md).
- **Retail & Hospitality**: Deploys serving and cleaning robots to optimize operations, improve guest experiences, and handle mundane workflow tasks (source: United Robotics Group _ Service robotics for your business.md).

### 2. Value Proposition
- **Labor Shortages**: The primary driver for service robot adoption is to alleviate chronic workforce shortages and increase job attractiveness by automating repetitive, physical labor (source: United Robotics Group _ Service robotics for your business.md).
