# Classifier Chain-based Pathological Test Recommendation

## Findings

This research introduces an AI-powered system designed to streamline and improve the accuracy of pathological test recommendations in medical diagnostics. By framing the task as a multi-label classification problem utilizing the Classifier Chain (CC) technique, the system leverages patient symptoms to predict necessary tests, thereby accelerating the diagnostic process and mitigating subjective physician interpretations. The study achieved high accuracy (98.83% with Logistic Regression + CC) and incorporated Explainable AI (XAI) methods, such as SHAP, to ensure transparency and clinical interpretability by detailing the contribution of each symptom to the recommended tests. This approach enhances confidence in the AI's reliability and can support physicians in critical decision-making.
