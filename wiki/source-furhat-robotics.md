## Main content

This source describes the capabilities and software architecture of the Furhat social robot platform (source: Welcome to Furhat Robotics.md).

### 1. Flagship HRI Research humanoid
- **Research Background**: Developed from years of human-robot interaction (HRI) research at KTH (Royal Institute of Technology), Furhat is a flagship social robot designed for research, education, and commercial use cases (source: Welcome to Furhat Robotics.md).

### 2. Back-Projected 3D Face Customization Engine
- **Physical Face Projection**: Unlike traditional humanoids with fixed mechanical masks, Furhat utilizes a back-projected 3D face engine combined with voice synthesis libraries (source: Welcome to Furhat Robotics.md).
- **Unlimited Customization**: This allows developers to project face textures of any age, ethnicity, or gender onto the physical head unit in real time (source: Welcome to Furhat Robotics.md).

### 3. Multi-User Face Tracking
- **Multi-Party Interactions**: Furhat is equipped with real-time camera-based face tracking that tracks multiple individuals simultaneously (source: Welcome to Furhat Robotics.md). This enables natural, multi-party interactions where the robot can shift its gaze and converse with several users in a shared space.

### 4. Software Suite: SDK & FurhatAI Creator
- **Furhat SDK**: Allows developers to build, test, and analyze conversational applications on their desktop (source: Welcome to Furhat Robotics.md).
- **FurhatAI Creator**: A platform to prompt specific robot personalities, control physical expressivity, connect custom APIs to create agentic workflows, and log interaction data (source: Welcome to Furhat Robotics.md).
