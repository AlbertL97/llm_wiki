## Main content

This source documents the launch of Chengdu's pilot program to integrate intelligent robots into public spaces and city operations (source: Chengdu robots are out of lab and on the streets, guiding traffic and speaking history.md).

### 1. Offline Edge Humanoid Traffic Directors
- **Intersection Deployment**: Humanoid robots are deployed at busy downtown intersections to assist traffic police in directing vehicles and guiding pedestrians (source: Chengdu robots are out of lab and on the streets, guiding traffic and speaking history.md).
- **Offline Edge Compute**: The robots perform their direction-guiding tasks completely offline, without requiring an active internet connection (source: Chengdu robots are out of lab and on the streets, guiding traffic and speaking history.md).

### 2. Holographic Museum Guides and AR Translation
- **Wu Hou Shrine Museum**: The city utilizes smart holographic screens to project ancient Three Kingdoms historical characters in naked-eye 3D (source: Chengdu robots are out of lab and on the streets, guiding traffic and speaking history.md).
- **Interactive Translation**: Visitors can chat with holographic guides and utilize AR smart glasses to receive real-time translations during their tour (source: Chengdu robots are out of lab and on the streets, guiding traffic and speaking history.md).

### 3. Primary School Safety Bots and Patrolling Dogs
- **Xiao Zha Safety Bot**: Styled after the traditional folk hero Nezha, a robot named Xiao Zha is deployed in primary schools to guide children on safety rules using interactive voice-based tips (source: Chengdu robots are out of lab and on the streets, guiding traffic and speaking history.md).
- **Street Patrol Dogs**: Autonomous robot dogs patrol Chengdu's downtown streets, scanning their surroundings to map and learn the environment in real time (source: Chengdu robots are out of lab and on the streets, guiding traffic and speaking history.md).

### 4. Economic Growth Targets
- **Core Industry Scale**: Chengdu targets expanding its core AI industry to a value exceeding 130 billion yuan by 2025, maintaining an annual growth rate of over 30% (source: Chengdu robots are out of lab and on the streets, guiding traffic and speaking history.md).
