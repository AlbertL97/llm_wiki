## Main content

This source introduces the **Center for Humane Technology (CHT)**, a nonprofit organization dedicated to ensuring that consequential technologies, such as artificial intelligence and social media, serve humanity (source: A better future with technology is possible..md).

Key details documented in the source include:
- **Exposing Persuasive Design**: CHT is dedicated to exposing the negative effects of persuasive technology and social media, empowering individuals to take action against manipulative design patterns (source: A better future with technology is possible..md).
- **Shifting Systemic Incentives**: The organization brings clarity to the operational mechanisms of the tech ecosystem to shift the core business and engagement incentives that drive it (source: A better future with technology is possible..md).
