## Main content

Qualitati is an AI-powered qualitative research platform designed for product, UX, marketing, HR, and academic teams, serving as an alternative to platforms like Outset.ai, Strella, and Listen Labs (source: AI User Research Platform _ Outset.ai & Strella Alternative.md).

### 1. Automated Data Collection
- **24/7 Interviews**: The platform deploys conversational AI agents to conduct qualitative user interviews via text or voice formats 24/7 (source: AI User Research Platform _ Outset.ai & Strella Alternative.md).
- **Conversational Surveys & Focus Groups**: AI acts as a moderator, facilitating interactive surveying and focus group discussions in multiple languages (source: AI User Research Platform _ Outset.ai & Strella Alternative.md).

### 2. Qualitative Text and Voice Analytics
- **Thematic Analysis**: The system automatically analyzes conversation transcripts to discover core themes, behavioral patterns, and qualitative codes (source: AI User Research Platform _ Outset.ai & Strella Alternative.md).
- **Voice Emotion Extraction**: Qualitati extracts acoustic features and emotional profiles directly from user audio recordings to analyze emotional responses (source: AI User Research Platform _ Outset.ai & Strella Alternative.md).

### 3. Academic and UX Reporting Support
- **Manuscript Review & Proofreading**: The AI provides writing support, assisting research teams in proofreading qualitative reports, drafting manuscripts, and formatting UX findings (source: AI User Research Platform _ Outset.ai & Strella Alternative.md).
