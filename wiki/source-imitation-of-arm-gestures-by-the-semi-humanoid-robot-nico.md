# Imitation of Arm Gestures by the Semi-Humanoid Robot NICO

This paper details the development of a gesture imitation system for the semi-humanoid robot NICO, aiming to enhance human-robot interaction (HRI).

## Key Findings

*   **Gesture Imitation System:** A novel system was developed to enable the semi-humanoid robot NICO to imitate human arm gestures.
*   **Technical Approach:** The system leverages analytical geometry and a pre-trained MediaPipe pose-estimation model to process monocular RGB input.
*   **Process:** It extracts 3D coordinates of human body landmarks, computes joint angles using geometric relations, and maps these angles to NICO's motor configuration for motion execution.
*   **Effectiveness:** Preliminary experiments with six participants demonstrated that the system can produce meaningful imitative motions for representative arm gestures.
*   **Limitations:** The study identified limitations in the system's ability to accurately imitate more complex poses and specific wrist-related movements.
