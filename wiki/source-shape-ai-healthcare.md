## Main content

### Overview
This medRxiv preprint documents the development and expert validation of the **SHAPE-AI** survey instrument. The survey evaluates clinician-AI performance, trust, cognitive load, and alert burden in healthcare settings (source: SHAPE-AI_ Development and Expert Validation of a Survey for Human-AI Performance Evaluation in Healthcare.md).

### Practical Application
1. **Alert Burden**: Addresses clinician burnout caused by interruptive alerts and alarm fatigue.
2. **Clinician Trust Calibration**: Validates metrics to evaluate clinician trust and appropriate reliance on decision-support systems (e.g., pediatric sepsis prediction models).
3. **Explainability**: Aligns post-hoc explainability features with clinical workflows, providing guidelines for validating decision support systems before deployment (source: SHAPE-AI_ Development and Expert Validation of a Survey for Human-AI Performance Evaluation in Healthcare.md).
