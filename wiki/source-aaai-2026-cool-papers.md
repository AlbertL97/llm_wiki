### Overview
This clipping catalogs accepted papers at the AAAI 2026 conference in the 'Humans and AI' track. It details key papers on adaptive ensembles, cooperation metrics, and explainability (source: AAAI.2026 - Humans and AI _ Cool Papers - Immersive Paper Discovery.md).

### Featured Papers and Concepts
1. **Adaptive Ensembles (Paper #1)**: *Align When They Want, Complement When They Need! Human-Centered Ensembles for Adaptive Human-AI Collaboration*.
   - **Tension**: A single AI model faces a trade-off: aligned AI builds trust by agreeing with human strengths but reinforces human errors; complementary AI boosts performance by covering human weaknesses but erodes trust by disagreeing with human strengths.
   - **Solution**: A human-centered adaptive ensemble that toggles between an **Aligned Specialist** and a **Complementary Specialist** based on context, utilizing a **Rational Routing Shortcut** mechanism.
2. **Inter-Dependencies in Teaming (Paper #2)**: *Who Is Helping Whom? Analyzing Inter-Dependencies to Evaluate Cooperation in Human-AI Teaming*. Maps causal dependencies between human and AI decisions to quantify team cooperation.
3. **Belief-Driven Value Alignment (Paper #24)**: *Belief-Driven Value Alignment for Human-Robot Collaboration*. Proposes models that align robotic action execution with human beliefs and preferences (source: AAAI.2026 - Humans and AI _ Cool Papers - Immersive Paper Discovery.md).
