## Main content

This source presents an overview of Generalist AI's progress in developing general-purpose robotic control models (source: An early preview of model capabilities _ Generalist.md).

### 1. General-Purpose Robot Mission
- **End-to-End AI Models**: Generalist AI aims to deploy general-purpose robots by training end-to-end neural network models rather than using partitioned or symbolic control loops (source: An early preview of model capabilities _ Generalist.md).
- **Dexterous Sensorimotor Policies**: The models are trained to learn sensorimotor policies that scale across different physical embodiments, diverse environments, and real-world physical interactions (source: An early preview of model capabilities _ Generalist.md).

### 2. Core Manipulation Demonstrations
Generalist AI showcases capabilities on manipulation tasks designed to stress specific performance areas (source: An early preview of model capabilities _ Generalist.md):
- **Fine Motor Control**: High-fidelity dexterity needed for delicate, detailed tasks.
- **Spatial and Temporal Precision**: Coordinating movements in 3D space with precise timing.
- **Cross-Robot Generalization**: Transferring trained policies across different robotic hardware setups and environmental settings.
- **Robustness to Disturbances**: Maintaining task execution despite unexpected external physical forces or disruptions.
