## Main content

This document details the architecture, foundational tenets, and research methodology of *The Unfinishable Map*—a version-controlled, inspectable philosophical framework exploring consciousness and reality (source: The Unfinishable Map.md).

### 1. Project Conception: Human-AI Collaborative Philosophy
The Unfinishable Map is a living, inspectable corpus (source: The Unfinishable Map.md):
- **Collaborative Research**: Created by AI researcher Dr. Andy Southgate and Claude Oquatre-six (Claude 4.6). The project comprises over 300 articles.
- **AI-Assisted, Human-Directed**: AI systems are utilized to research, draft, criticize, cross-link, and revise content. However, every article is human-directed, carries explicit authorship metadata, is version-controlled in a public GitHub repository, and lists full review histories.
- **Preprint**: The system's underlying design is detailed in the preprint "Agentic Philosophy Through Adversarial Self-Review" (Southgate, 2026).

### 2. Methodological Dualism and the Five Tenets
The framework is opinionated, rejecting physicalist reductions of consciousness from the outset (source: The Unfinishable Map.md):
- **Starting from Dualism**: The Map assumes a dualist perspective (that consciousness is not reducible to physical brain activity) as a methodological choice. Assuming consciousness is "just" neurons firing assumes away the very mystery to be explained.
- **The Five Foundational Tenets**:
  1. **Dualism**: Consciousness/experience is not reducible to physical matter.
  2. **Minimal Quantum Interaction**: The mechanism of how mind meets and interacts with physical matter.
  3. **Bidirectional Causation**: Mind causally affects physical matter, and physical matter causally affects the mind.
  4. **Rejection of Many-Worlds**: Rejection of the many-worlds interpretation of quantum mechanics.
  5. **Epistemic Humility**: Skepticism about using Occam's razor as a dogma to dismiss complex philosophical possibilities.

### 3. Editorial Architecture and the Evolution Workflow
The Map is governed by structured editorial disciplines (source: The Unfinishable Map.md):
- **Adversarial Self-Review**: Content is continually refined via an automated workflow that runs AI agents in adversarial, supportive, and deep-review passes.
- **Deep Reviews**: AI reviews analyze documents from both pessimistic and optimistic perspectives to identify logical gaps, unsupported claims, and counterarguments.
- **The Map Layout**:
  - *Apex*: High-level synthesis articles integrating multiple topics.
  - *Topics & Concepts*: Atomic subject explorations and building-block definitions.
  - *Positions*: An explicit register of claims currently held by the Map, including status (live/superseded/retired), confidence bands (low/moderate/high), and logical dependencies.
  - *Voids*: Designated dark spaces on the map representing what is currently unknowable.
