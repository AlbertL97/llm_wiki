# The Impact of Security and Privacy Controls on Users' Emotional Engagement with Generative AI Chatbots

## Overview
This article investigates how the presence and nature of security and privacy (S&P) controls affect users' willingness to engage in emotionally sensitive disclosure with generative AI chatbots, which are increasingly used for emotional support and companionship.

## Key Findings
*   **Dual Role of Chatbots:** Generative AI chatbots are becoming popular for emotional support and companionship.
*   **S&P Control Impact:** The study examined how various S&P controls influence user attitudes towards emotionally sensitive disclosure.
*   **Effectiveness of Deletion Controls:** Controls allowing users to delete their disclosures had the most significant positive impact on engagement and willingness to disclose.
*   **Comprehension Challenges:** More technically advanced controls, such as local-only processing and model training opt-outs, were less effective because participants found them difficult to understand.
*   **Fragile Trust:** Despite the implementation of S&P controls, users' trust in these systems remains fragile, with doubts about whether the controls function as promised.
*   **Recommendations:** The research proposes actionable recommendations to improve user comprehension of S&P controls, build credible assurances, and appropriately set barriers for users, especially those in distress.

## Methodology
*   A mixed-methods vignette study was conducted with 354 participants from the U.S.
